# Weather Vessel Notes

The clay pot can introduce the weather card system through simple states of holding and condition.

## Vessel states

- open
- closed
- full
- empty
- side / tilted
- broken
- patterned

These states can become an embodied and material bridge into weather noticing.

A vessel can teach:
containment,
exposure,
gathering,
release,
overflow,
damage,
surface trace,
and pattern.

This is not meteorology reduced to symbols.
It is weather language approached through a familiar form of holding.
