# 🌐 DiscoveryMode Commons
## From the Field · Signals from the Field

---

## 🌱 Purpose

A living space where real moments are shared.

Not polished lessons.  
Not perfect outcomes.  
Not products.

But:

- what was seen  
- what was tried  
- what shifted  

No paywalls. No gatekeeping.

> Teachers helping teachers.  
> Students helping teachers.  
> Community woven into the practice.

---

## 🧭 Core Anchor

**State → Action → Tool**

- ↓ Downward · ↔ Sideways · → Onward  
- Plant · Tend · Repair · Connect  

The sequence holds the system together.  
Everything else can evolve.

---

## ✍️ Contribution Format (Required)

Every entry must include:

### 1. What I Saw
State of the room / field

### 2. What I Tried
Action → Tool

### 3. What Shifted
What changed (even slightly)

---

### 🌿 Context Reminder (include in every entry)

*This worked here, in this moment, with these people. It may not transfer directly.*

---

## 🌿 Example Entry

**What I Saw**  
Unanchored room. Some agitated, some drifting, some absent.

**What I Tried**  
Called MosquitoMode → didn’t land  
Re-read → Tend  
Opened DiscoveryDome quietly: “Oh… what’s this?”  
Shifted to WeatherCards

**What Shifted**  
Students drew closer  
Attention gathered around a small object  
Breathing settled  
Room became readable again

## 🌐 Signals from the Field

The classroom receives signals from:

- community (parents, neighbors, visitors)  
- place (animals, weather, environment)  
- students (objects, observations, questions)  

Examples:

- a nest spotted in a park  
- a rhinoceros beetle under leaves  
- a visiting musician  
- a mural needing hands  

---

### 🧭 Signal Test

A signal is something the room **naturally orients toward**.

You’ll notice:

- heads turn without prompting  
- attention gathers  
- energy shifts  
- curiosity appears  

---

### ⚠️ Not a Signal (yet)

If you have to:

- convince  
- repeat  
- amplify  

…it’s not a signal.

---

> Signals are followed, not imposed.

---

## ⚠️ Drift Warning

You may have stopped reading the field if:

- you reach for a tool immediately  
- you repeat a move louder when it fails  
- you try to “get through” the plan  
- you override what the room is showing you  

---

### 🔁 Correction

Pause. Re-read.  
Return to **State → Action → Tool**

---
