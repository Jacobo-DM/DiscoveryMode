Story Seed – Luna and Maimi

Maimi bursts into Luna’s room laughing.

“You’re not going to believe this.”

She demonstrates Mosquito Mode, swatting imaginary insects and jumping
around the room.

Luna stares for a moment and then bursts into laughter.

Maimi lowers her voice.

“I think there’s a mosquito in here… one of those dragon-sized ones
my grandma says live in the high country.”

Luna’s eyes widen.

“The high country? That’s where all the strange things start happening…”

One day their curiosity will lead them toward the Monster Kitchen.
