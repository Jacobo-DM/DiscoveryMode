# Story Seeds

Story seeds are living starting points.

They are not full curriculum, fixed modules, or completed explanations.
They are generative forms: images, objects, gestures, questions, and small architectures that can open inquiry.

In DiscoveryMode, a story seed may begin with a clay pot, a weather pattern, a circle, a sound, a pocket object, a local material, or a simple act of noticing.

Story seeds are meant to be portable, adaptable, and alive to place.

## What lives here

This folder holds foundational seeds that can grow into lessons, field practices, local adaptations, or bridges to other parts of the ecosystem.

The first seed cluster here is:

- `vessels/` — clay pots, local forms of holding, weather states, sound, breakage, and return
