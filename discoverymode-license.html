The Feral Garden Clause

The Feral Garden Clause ensures that DiscoveryMode cannot be privatized, centralized, or extracted into commercial control. It requires:

  • No one may trademark or claim exclusive rights over DiscoveryMode.
  • No paywall, subscription, franchise, or certification pathway may restrict access to the practice.
  • Local communities may adapt, remix, rename, and evolve it according to their needs.
  • Stewardship is shared, distributed, and accountable to the people who practice it.

DiscoveryMode must remain a living, community-rooted, non-commercial practice.
