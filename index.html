DiscoveryMode
⌇ ⌇

The illusion of modern knowledge is that the world can be held entirely in the head. But nothing truly breathes there.

Understanding begins when the whole body — senses, instincts, the quiet intelligence of being — enters into conversation with the world.

DiscoveryMode is what that conversation looks like in practice. It is a return to the state of wonder from which our most profound questions arise.
