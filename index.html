DiscoveryMode

A garden for learning, listening, movement, and return.

DiscoveryMode is a way of learning that begins with the body, the place, and the stories we carry. It is a commons practice — not a brand, not a product, not a market.

Full README: View README.md

The Garden Ethic

DiscoveryMode grows like a garden that refuses fences. It spreads through invitation, not ownership.

  • Anyone may tend it.
  • No one may claim it.
  • Every community adapts it to its climate, culture, and needs.

Its strength is diversity. Its purpose is remembering what learning feels like when breath, body, wonder, and place are allowed to guide us.
