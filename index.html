DiscoveryMode
⌇ ⌇

The illusion of modern knowledge is that the world can be held entirely in the head. But nothing truly breathes there.

Understanding begins when the whole body — senses, instincts, the quiet intelligence of being — enters into conversation with the world.

DiscoveryMode is what that conversation looks like in practice. It is a return to the state of wonder from which our most profound questions arise.

Racoon Lantern

A quiet discovery pattern. Move slowly through the dark field. The page waits; attention reveals.

The lantern follows the paw.

This is not a digital firework. It is a small way to make attention tactile again — useful for hidden questions, field prompts, story crumbs, and gentle discovery.

The lantern must reward noticing, not addiction. It must never hide essential content.

Notice what was already here. Move slowly. What changes when you look with your whole body? The page does not shout. It waits. A small question can be a doorway.

Use sparingly. Keep the full meaning available without the effect. Let the lantern deepen discovery, not block access.
